About Us!

Aiming to deepen the connection between bees and humans, Happy Bees Honey Farm is a family-owned, organic sanctuary for liquid gold-making bees sprawling across 50 acres of pristine land. Our primary purpose is to craft the purest organic honey while fostering the health of bees and the environment. We specialize in ethical bee-keeping practices to help our little bees create honey that captures natureâ€™s essence. Our products are certified organic and sustainably produced, upholding the highest purity level, and packed with enzymes, antioxidants, and pollen for maximum health benefits. Our farm shares three delicious variations, Orange Blossom, Gallberry (Wildflower), and Allergy Blend, available for purchase.

Excellent for:


  • Health-Conscious Consumers: Those who value the purity and holistic benefits of organic raw honey.
  • Local Supporters: Community members who prioritize locally sourced products, actively engage in sustainable practices, and are eager to support local agriculture.
  • Natural Remedy Seekers: Individuals who appreciate honey's medicinal properties and wellness potential.
  • Thoughtful Gifters: People who embrace eco-friendly gifting and support ethical farming.
  • Sustainability Advocates: Individuals interested in supporting environmentally friendly practices.
